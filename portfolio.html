Previous Projects

lucyejwoods.co.uk

This project was undertaken at the bequest of Lucy Woods, an established energy and environment journalist.

The original purpose of the site was to act as a platform upon which Lucy's best articles could be showcased. The site would need to act both as a business card and a portfolio. To this end, a single-page site with an always-visible bio and contact details panel would sit aside a timeline of published works.

Since the original brief, the scope of the site has since expanded to include a (currently rudimentary) bespoke blogging platform.

The blogging platform is currently undergoing the final stages of deployment. The next stage of the site will involve combining Lucy's various social media feeds into a single timeline

An extensive case study of this project can be read in this (coming soon) blog post.

tektondpw.co.uk

TektonDPW is a long-standing architecture and design firm based in West London. Designing and developing their website required an insight into the similar process that beings a building's life.

Using the existing company art assets a design was drawn up that would seamlessly blend with the existing branding. SVG and CSS assets were created to provide the highest fidelity imagery, worthy of a design firm.

With a strong set of specifications for what information would need to be provided and prioritied, the website is currently being developed into a fully responsive experience.
